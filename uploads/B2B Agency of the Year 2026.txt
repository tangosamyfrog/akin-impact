Perspective Made Real: Pods of Impact
CONFIDENTIAL | FOR JUDGING PURPOSES ONLY
We believed caregiving infrastructure was an untapped brand channel. Then we built the proof.

Pods of Impact is AKIN's perspective in product form. Backed by NVPC and Temasek Foundation, POI converts nursing pod infrastructure into a purpose-built OOH network, allowing brands to reach mothers during high-dwell, high-trust care moments. Unlike mass-media OOH, the environment is captive and contextually earned.
This is not a CSR initiative. It is a structural argument about where attention lives in markets that mass media misses — made real through the Go!Mama and AKIN ecosystems.
Proof point: 20,000+ community members supported, 300 nursing pods targeted by 2027, 45+ impact partners activated
